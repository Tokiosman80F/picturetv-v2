Meet Mala Seza

Mala Seza is the Founder and Executive Producer of Picture TV Media — a Visibility Infrastructure Company based in New York City that turns event coverage, brand interviews, and strategic storytelling into measurable media assets that convert attention into real revenue.

With over 6 million views, 9,000+ subscribers, coverage of 150+ events, and a platform featuring 500+ brands, Mala brings both the vision and the track record to every project he touches.

He is also the host of Mala Speak Sunday — a weekly YouTube talk show exploring business, culture, visibility, and community — and the founder of CiTiSweEP, a community development organization focused on youth empowerment, education, and sustainable futures.
